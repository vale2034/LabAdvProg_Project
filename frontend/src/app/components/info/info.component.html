The following ecommerce-based website is produced by Valerio Botte and Marco Cardinali for the exam of the course of Laboratory of Advanced Programming.

Our goal is to create a simple alternative to the vast array of online shops that typically lack sensitivity in controlling the quality of the products sold.

A typical example is online fast fashion shops. We prioritize an e-commerce platform that leads in selling eco-sustainable wardrobe products, setting a reference for all those who wish to buy clothes online, like Zalando.

We provide users the option to choose their delivery method and offer information on the type of products they will purchase from our site.

The most important future development for this project is the implementation of certification for products with the correct logos, giving users a more direct understanding of what they are buying.

Considering the significant challenge in our society to safeguard the environment, our vision is to influence people's purchasing habits in a positive way.
